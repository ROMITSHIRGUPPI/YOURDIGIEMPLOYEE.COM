How it works

Three steps to a paperless office

Install once. Configure in 30 minutes. Every memo from that point is digital, tracked, and traceable.

STEP 01
⚡
Install and run selfSetup()
One menu click creates all 12 sheets, installs 5 automated triggers, generates your portal QR code, and configures the entire system — automatically. Zero manual configuration.
STEP 02
📱
Staff scan the portal QR
Print and display the portal QR on your noticeboard. One QR for both Official Memos and Call Memos — staff choose their form on a clean choice screen.
STEP 03
✅
Approvals happen in email
Approvers receive a cryptographically-signed email with Approve and Reject buttons. One click — decision recorded, submitter notified, MASTER MEMO updated. Automatically.
Features

Everything your workflow needs

Built for industrial organisations where reliability, traceability, and zero downtime are non-negotiable.

📋
Official Memo Form
Material Design 3 HTML form with priority chips, live DP-01 validation, CC support, Drive attachment links, and a 3-step preview flow.
📱
Call Memo via QR
Workers scan a QR code, verify via OTP magic link, and submit a Call Memo with up to 30 workers selected from a live designation-filtered list.
🔔
Auto Escalation
Stage 1 reminder at 24h. Stage 2 HOD alert at 48h. Fully automated — no manual follow-up, no missed deadlines.
📊
Command Center
Live dashboard showing pending, approved, rejected, and escalated counts. Daily health report emailed to admin automatically every morning.
🔄
Retry Queue
Failed emails automatically retry with exponential backoff — 1 min, 2 min, 4 min — before alerting the admin. Zero lost notifications.
📡
Server-Independent
Runs on Google's infrastructure. Staff submit memos on mobile data even when your office network, server, or power is completely down.

Security

Enterprise-grade security,
zero extra cost

Every approval link is cryptographically signed. Every OTP is SHA-256 hashed. Verified clean by Google's CASA security programme — Tier 2 and Tier 3 not triggered.

✓ CASA Security Verified — All Tiers Clean
🔐
HMAC-SHA256 Token Signing
Every approval link is cryptographically bound to the approver's email, memo ID, decision type, and timestamp. Any tampering invalidates the link instantly.
🔑
OTP Authentication
6-digit OTP stored as SHA-256 hash only — never plain text. Expires in 5 minutes. Single-use. 5-attempt brute-force limit per 10 minutes per user.
🛡️
Zero Restricted Scopes
No GmailApp, no DriveApp, no CardService. Six non-restricted OAuth scopes only. CSP-compliant HTML with no inline event handlers across all pages.
🔒
Data Stays in Your Account
All data lives in your organisation's own Google Sheets. The developer has zero access to any deployment, by design. Architecturally impossible to breach from outside.
