How It Works

Three steps to a paperless office

Install once. Configure in 30 minutes. Every memo from that point is digital, tracked, and traceable.

01
⚡
Install and run selfSetup()
One menu click creates all 12 sheets, installs 5 automated triggers, generates your portal QR code, and configures the system — automatically.
02
📱
Staff scan the QR code
Print and display the portal QR on your noticeboard. Staff scan it to submit Official Memos. Workers scan it to submit Call Memos with OTP authentication.
03
✅
Approvals happen in email
Approvers receive a secure email with Approve and Reject buttons. One click — decision recorded, submitter notified, MASTER MEMO updated automatically.
Features

Everything your memo workflow needs

Built specifically for industrial organisations where reliability, traceability, and zero downtime are not optional.

📋
Official Memo Form
Material Design 3 HTML form with priority chips, live DP-01 validation, CC support, Drive attachment links, and a 3-step preview flow.
📱
Call Memo via QR
Workers scan a QR code on their phone, verify via OTP magic link, and submit a Call Memo with up to 30 workers selected from a live list.
🔔
Auto Escalation
Memos not actioned within 24 hours trigger a reminder. After 48 hours the HOD is automatically alerted. No manual follow-up needed.

Command Center
Live dashboard showing pending, approved, rejected, and escalated memo counts. Daily health report emailed to admin automatically.

Retry Queue
Failed emails are automatically retried with exponential backoff before alerting the admin. Zero lost notifications, ever.

Works When Server is Down
Runs on Google's infrastructure. Staff submit memos on mobile data even when your office network, server, or power is down.

Security

Enterprise-grade security, zero extra cost

Every approval link is cryptographically signed. Every token is single-use. Every OTP is SHA-256 hashed. Verified by Google's CASA security programme.

🔐
HMAC-SHA256 Token Signing
Every approval and rejection link is cryptographically bound to the approver's email, memo ID, decision type, and timestamp. Tamper-proof by design.
🔑
OTP Authentication
6-digit OTP stored as SHA-256 hash only — never plain text. Expires in 5 minutes. Single-use. 5 attempt brute-force limit per 10 minutes.
🛡️
CASA Security Verified
Zero restricted OAuth scopes. No GmailApp, no DriveApp, no CardService. CASA Tier 1 pass. Tier 2 and Tier 3 not triggered. CSP compliant HTML.
🔒
Data Stays in Your Account
All data lives in your organisation's own Google Sheets. The developer has zero access to any deployment data. Architecturally impossible to access.
