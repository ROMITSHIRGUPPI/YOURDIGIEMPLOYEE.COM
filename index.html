Process

Three steps. Fully automated.

Install once. Every memo after that is tracked, routed, and resolved without any manual work.

STEP 01
⚡
Run selfSetup() once
One click from the ⚙ DMS-IA menu creates all 12 sheets, installs 5 time-based triggers, generates the unified portal QR code, and builds the SETTINGS dashboard. Zero manual configuration.
STEP 02
📱
Staff and workers submit via QR
One QR code on the factory noticeboard opens a choice screen. Official Memo for staff (OTP-authenticated HTML form). Call Memo for workers (designation-filtered list, up to 30 people). No app download required.
STEP 03
✅
Approvals happen in email
Approver clicks Approve or Reject in their email. Decision recorded, submitter notified, AUDIT LOG updated — immediately. If no action: auto-escalation fires at 24h, then HOD is alerted at 48h.
Features

Everything your workflow needs

Built for industrial organisations where reliability, traceability, and zero downtime are non-negotiable requirements.

📋
Official Memo Form
Material Design 3 HTML form — priority chips, live DP-01 validation, CC support, Google Drive attachment links, 3-step preview and confirm flow.
📱
Call Memo via QR
Workers scan QR → enter DP → receive OTP magic link → select from designation-filtered worker list → submit. Up to 30 workers per memo, server-side enforced.
⏰
Automatic Escalation
Stage 1 reminder to approver at 24h. Stage 2 HOD alert at 48h. Configurable per deployment from SETTINGS sheet. Completely unattended.
📊
Command Center
Live dashboard — pending, approved, rejected, and escalated memo counts by department. Daily health report emailed to admin every morning automatically.
🔄
Retry Queue
Failed email sends retry with exponential backoff — 1 min, 2 min, 4 min — before alerting the admin. Zero silent failures, ever.
🖨️
PDF Print View
Every approved memo has a branded print view for physical filing. Full memo details, company name, department, decision, and timestamp. Professionally formatted.

Security

Cryptographic security.
No extras needed.

Every link is signed. Every OTP is hashed. Verified by Google's CASA security programme. All data stays inside your own Google account — the developer has zero access.

✓ CASA Security Verified — Tier 1 passed · Tier 2 not triggered · Tier 3 not triggered
🔐
HMAC-SHA256 Signing
Every approval link is cryptographically bound to the approver's email, memo ID, and timestamp. Tampering invalidates it immediately.
🔑
OTP — SHA-256 Hashed
Raw OTP never stored. SHA-256 hash only. Expires in 5 minutes. Single-use. 5-attempt brute-force limit per 10 minutes.
🛡️
Zero Restricted Scopes
No GmailApp. No DriveApp. No CardService. Six non-restricted OAuth scopes only. CSP-compliant HTML throughout.
🏢
Data Stays Yours
All data lives in your own Google Sheets. No backend. No external server. The developer cannot access any deployment data.
