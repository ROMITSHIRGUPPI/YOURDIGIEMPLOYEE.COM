What is Your Digi Employee?

Your Digi Employee is a suite of digital workplace automation tools built entirely on Google Workspace. Every manual system in your organisation — paper memos, manual approvals, handwritten registers — is replaced by a fast, secure, and trackable digital process.

No servers. No installation. No IT department needed. Everything runs inside your existing Google account.

🚀 DMS-IA — Digital Memo System Industrial Automation

Our first product. Built for industrial organisations managing internal memo workflows across departments.

  • ✅ Official Memo submission via secure HTML web form
  • ✅ Call Memo via QR code with OTP authentication
  • ✅ HMAC-secured approval and rejection email links
  • ✅ Automatic escalation to HOD after deadline
  • ✅ Command Center dashboard and daily health reports
  • ✅ Supports up to 3,000 workers per deployment
  • ✅ Works even when your company server is down
