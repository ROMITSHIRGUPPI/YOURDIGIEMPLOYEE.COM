Process

Three steps. Fully automated.

Install once. Every memo after that is tracked, routed, and resolved automatically.

→
STEP 01
⚡
Run selfSetup() once
One click from the ⚙ DMS-IA menu creates all 12 sheets, installs 5 time-based triggers, and generates the portal QR code. Zero manual configuration.
→
STEP 02
📱
Staff and workers submit via QR
One QR code on the noticeboard opens a choice screen. Official Memo for staff. Call Memo (OTP-authenticated) for factory workers. No app download.
STEP 03
✅
Approvals happen in email
Approver clicks Approve or Reject in their email. One click — memo status updated, submitter notified, AUDIT LOG recorded. If no action: auto-escalation fires.
Features

Everything your memo workflow needs

📋
Official Memo Form
Material Design 3 HTML form — priority chips, live DP validation, CC support, Drive attachment link, 3-step preview flow.
📱
Call Memo via QR
Workers scan QR → enter DP → receive OTP → fill form with designation-filtered worker list → submit. Up to 30 workers per memo.
⏰
Auto Escalation
Stage 1: Reminder to approver at 24h. Stage 2: HOD email at 48h. Configurable per deployment from SETTINGS sheet.
📊
Command Center
Live dashboard — pending, approved, rejected, escalated counts. Daily health report emailed to admin every morning automatically.
🔄
Retry Queue
Failed email sends retry with exponential backoff (1→2→4 min). After 3 failures, admin is alerted. Zero silent failures.
🖨️
PDF Print View
Every memo has a branded print view for physical filing. Same design language as email — company name, logo colour, full memo details.
Security

Cryptographic security.
No extras needed.

Every link is signed. Every OTP is hashed. Verified by Google's CASA programme. All data stays in your own Google account.

✓ CASA Security Verified — Tier 1 passed, Tier 2 not triggered, Tier 3 not triggered. Zero restricted OAuth scopes. CSP-compliant HTML.
🔐
HMAC-SHA256 Token Signing
Every approval link is cryptographically bound to the approver's email, memo ID, and timestamp. Any tampering invalidates it immediately.
🔑
OTP — SHA-256 Hashed
Raw OTP never stored — only its SHA-256 hash. Expires in 5 minutes. Single-use. 5-attempt brute-force limit per 10 minutes.
🛡️
Zero Restricted Scopes
No GmailApp. No DriveApp. No CardService. Six non-restricted scopes only. Passes CASA Tier 2 automatically with no security assessment needed.
🏢
Your Data Stays Yours
All data lives in your organisation's own Google Sheets. The developer has zero access. Architecturally impossible — no backend, no database, no external server.
